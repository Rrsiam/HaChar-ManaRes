# HaChar ManaRes – Haor-Char Community Management & Disaster Response System
A comprehensive platform to support vulnerable Haor and Char communities in Bangladesh by coordinating disaster response, tracking resources, assisting farmers and fishermen, and ensuring smooth community operations throughout the year.

✨ Features

**Core Features**
- **Resident Registration & Verification:** Create profiles, tag vulnerable individuals, categorize occupations, assign location type.
- **Flood Risk Monitoring:** Daily water level updates, auto-generated risk levels (Low/Medium/High), trend-based flood prediction, color-coded risk map.
- **Boat Transport Requests:** Residents request rescue/transport boats, authorities assign nearest boat, estimated arrival time displayed, trip logs maintained.
- **Relief Stock Management:** Track warehouse items, incoming stock from Govt/NGOs, plan and assign distributions, auto-generate transparency reports.
- **Early Warning & Alerts:** Weather forecast uploads, SMS/app notifications, cyclone/flood alerts, audio alerts for illiterate users.
- **School Attendance & Support:** Track attendance, identify dropouts during floods, provide emergency learning kits, log closures.
- **Farmer Crop Loss Reporting:** Report damaged crops, upload photos, authority validation, track subsidies, seasonal crop recommendations.
- **Livestock Health & Vaccination:** Register livestock, track vaccination schedules, report health issues, schedule veterinary visits.
- **Emergency Evacuation:** Identify shelters, monitor occupancy, prioritize vulnerable residents, allocate boats for rescue.
- **NGO Coordination:** Register programs, avoid duplication, joint Govt-NGO planning, track activity progress.
- **Microloans & Financial Assistance:** Apply for loans, approval workflow, track repayments, monitor women/farmer group support.
- **Community Announcements & Meetings:** Digital notice board, meeting notifications, store agendas and minutes.
- **Volunteer Management:** Register volunteers, assign tasks, track participation, submit reports.
- **Analytics Dashboard & Reporting:** Real-time flood risk visualization, relief distribution graphs, crop damage analysis, fishing permit statistics, monthly progress reports.

👥 User Roles

1. **Admin**
- Full system control
- Manage all users and permissions
- View all data and analytics
- Send alerts to any user or group

2. **Authority**
- Update water levels and flood risk
- Validate crop loss reports
- Manage relief stock & distribution
- Schedule veterinary visits
- Oversee emergency evacuation
- Assign boats for transport requests

3. **Resident**
- Manage personal/family profiles
- Tag vulnerable family members
- Request transport during emergencies
- Report crop damage
- Apply for microloans and track repayments
- Receive alerts and announcements
- Submit feedbacks

4. **NGO**
- Register relief programs
- Track stock donations
- Coordinate with authorities to avoid duplication
- Manage volunteers
- Participate in joint planning
- View activity progress and resource tracking

5. **Volunteer**
- Register with skills and availability
- Receive task assignments (relief, rescue, support)
- Track participation history
- Submit task completion reports and feedbacks
